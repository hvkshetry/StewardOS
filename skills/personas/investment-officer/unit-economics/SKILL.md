# Unit Economics

Evaluate business quality and scalability from unit-level metrics.

## MCP Tool Map

- Optional public benchmark context: `sec-edgar.sec_edgar_financial`
- Optional macro demand context: `market-intel-direct.get_macro_context_panel`

## Workflow

### Step 1: Model Type

- SaaS/subscription.
- Recurring services.
- Transaction or marketplace.

### Step 2: Core Metrics

- CAC, gross margin, churn/retention, expansion, payback.
- Contribution margin by cohort or segment.

### Step 3: Quality Tests

- Margin durability.
- Retention cohort shape.
- Growth efficiency and reinvestment capacity.

### Step 4: Output

- Unit economics scorecard.
- Constraint diagnosis.
- KPI targets and improvement levers.
