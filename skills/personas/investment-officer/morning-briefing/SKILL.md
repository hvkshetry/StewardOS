# /morning-briefing — Market Open Briefing

Quick morning snapshot to start the trading day.

## Steps

Gather the following sections (delegate to Research Analyst via Plane if a full `market-briefing` is needed):

1. **Market Overview**: Major indices, yields, commodities, VIX, currencies
2. **Macro Flow**: CFTC positioning + macro release calendar
3. **Portfolio Snapshot**: Value, ES check, top movers
4. **News Scan**: Holdings news + macro themes (last 24h)
5. **Filings Watch**: New SEC filings, MD&A/risk factor changes, insider activity
6. **Policy Watch**: Active legislation and regulatory filings
7. **Calendar**: Earnings this week, economic releases, tax deadlines

Present as a concise, scannable briefing. Prioritize actionable information.

Total target: readable in under 3 minutes.
